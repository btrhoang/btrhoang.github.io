folder [Sugar]  

Growing up in a single-parent household, I have somewhat a special emotional attachment to orphaned children. At SUGAR, I have got the chance to interact with and add values to the lives of children at shelters. Leading a network of hundreds of volunteers and enlisting support from our 33,000 followers on Facebook, I’m contented that I can help take care of them both mentally and financially.

Regular trips to orphanages have brought home to me many heartful values. Although seemingly lacking in what others deemed as a “real” father or mother figure, all the kids showed love and care for me in the most charming way possible. Every time I arrive, they welcome with a liveshow of our fav songs. And when it’s time for me to go back home, they help me wear my jackets, and remind me of my belongings since I’m awfully absent-minded.

My members and I genuinely consider them our brothers and sisters, and I’m sure they also do.
